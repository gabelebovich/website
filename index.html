Understanding the Role of Social Connections in the Formation of Political Coalitions

• Using an instrumental variable approach, I find that Democrat-leaning counties (above-median Democrat-net-Republican percentage) respond robustly to the influence of their social network in all years examined. Republican-leaning counties accelerated coalition formation in years after the 2012 election.

Treatment Heterogeneity in the Effect of Right to Carry Laws on Crime: A Machine Learning Approach

• Using several generalized random forests we find that counties with high levels of crime benefit from right-to-carry laws, however crime levels rise in below-median crime-level counties.
